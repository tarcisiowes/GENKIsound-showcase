# Roadmap

## Near Term

- Hardening pass for background audio and notification behavior on real Android and iOS devices
- Finalize the practical Apple Music scope on iOS
- Continue stabilizing streaming and playback flows in real device conditions

## Mid Term

- Subscription entitlement layer
- More complete premium and paywall architecture
- Better telemetry around performance and playback quality
- Stronger personalization around music-learning behavior

## Later

- AI-assisted preset suggestions
- More advanced personalization
- Broader music-learning loops and content depth

## Public Showcase Notes

This roadmap is intentionally public-facing. It highlights direction, not internal implementation detail.
